Estavius Von Gloopenheimer:

Spellcaster seeking salvation

Who is he?

Estavius Von Gloopenheimer is an ancient wizard and former member of a shady secret society. After plotting an assassination of his king, he was cursed by the higher beings of his world and banished. He seeks every day for a chance to atone for his actions, and he's this close to going on a time-hopping journey to take down his former colleagues!

What does he do?

Estavius takes down his foes in combat by powering up his allies, summoning fantastical creatures, and slow-roasting his enemies with damage over time.

Fun Fact!

Estavius' character began as a silly sketch, which is the basis for his design and what you see on this page!
