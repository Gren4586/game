What is Time Game?

Time Game follows the story of a disgraced wizard from the past and a law-breaking cyborg from the future teaming up to take down evils spanning generations!

How does it play?

Time Game is set to feature unique combat mechanics that change depending on when, where, and who you are!

Is "Time Game" really its name?

Luckily, no! This is simply a placeholder name, as the project hasn't been given a proper title as of yet.
